About Bigfoot Believers United

Bigfoot Believers United was founded in 2021 by avid Sasquatch enthusiasts seeking to shed light on the truth about these mysterious creatures. After many years spent independently tracking, researching, and analyzing alleged Bigfoot sightings and evidence, we decided to come together as one community.

We aim to be a judgment-free zone focused on facts, science, respect, and sometimes, a bit of humor!

As the premier network for connecting fellow Bigfoot devotees, we host regular member forums, organize field research expeditions, compile databases of incidents, and serve as a hub for genuine Bigfoot-related media and information sources.
